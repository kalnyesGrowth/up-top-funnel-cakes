Our Story

Born from a Love of Flavor

Up Top Funnel Cakes started in 2021 with a simple idea: take the classic funnel cake and elevate it into something extraordinary. What began as a passion project quickly became one of Clinton, Maryland's most sought-after dessert experiences.

Every funnel cake is made fresh to order. No pre-made batches, no shortcuts. We pour the batter, fry it golden, and load it with premium toppings right in front of you. That's the Up Top difference.

As a proud Black-owned business, we bring culture and creativity to every order. From Strawberry Cheesecake to Banana Pudding to our signature Fruity Pebble Stuffle, each creation reflects our commitment to bold flavors and quality ingredients.

Questions

Frequently Asked

Do I need to order ahead or can I walk in?
You can order online through our Clover ordering system for pickup, or order delivery through DoorDash and Uber Eats. We recommend ordering ahead to ensure your funnel cake is hot and fresh when you arrive.
How long does it take to make a funnel cake?
Each funnel cake is made fresh to order. Preparation typically takes 10-15 minutes depending on the toppings and current order volume. We never pre-make our funnel cakes, so everything arrives hot and crispy.
Do you cater events?
Yes. We cater birthdays, weddings, corporate events, block parties, and more. We bring the full funnel cake experience to your location. Call us at (202) 766-2584 to discuss your event details and get a custom quote.
What's your most popular funnel cake?
The Strawberry Cheesecake is our top seller and a fan favorite. Customers also love the Banana Pudding and Red Velvet. If you're new, any of the signature funnel cakes at $22 are a great way to experience what makes Up Top different.
What delivery area do you cover?
We're based in Clinton, MD and deliver through DoorDash and Uber Eats. Delivery range depends on the platform, but we typically serve the greater Clinton, Waldorf, and surrounding Maryland areas. For in-store pickup, order through our Clover system.
